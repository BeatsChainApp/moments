## ğŸ‘¥ Test Groups

### Group 1: Community Users (General Public)
**Size:** 20-30 people  
**Profile:** Everyday South Africans from different provinces, ages 25-55, mix of urban/rural

**Testing Focus:**
- Subscription flow (START command)
- Region selection (REGIONS command)
- Interest preferences (INTERESTS command)
- Receiving and reading Moments
- Understanding attribution blocks
- Unsubscribe flow (STOP command)
- Sharing community content (sending messages)
- Privacy and data concerns

**Success Criteria:**
- 80%+ successfully subscribe
- 70%+ select their regions correctly
- 60%+ share at least one community moment
- 90%+ understand how to unsubscribe
- 75%+ report feeling the content is relevant

---

### Group 2: Authority Users (Community Leaders)
**Size:** 10-15 people  
**Profile:** School principals, community leaders, NGO coordinators, local government officials

**Testing Focus:**
- Authority request flow (REQUEST AUTHORITY command)
- Multi-step verification process
- Creating Moments via admin dashboard
- Understanding trust levels and attribution
- Sponsored content creation
- Broadcast scheduling
- Analytics and engagement metrics

**Success Criteria:**
- 90%+ complete authority request successfully
- 80%+ create and broadcast at least one Moment
- 70%+ understand trust level system
- 85%+ find admin dashboard intuitive
- 75%+ report value in analytics

---

### Group 3: Mixed Scenario Testing
**Size:** 15-20 people  
**Profile:** Mix of community users and potential authority users

**Testing Focus:**
- Real-world usage patterns
- Edge cases and error handling
- Multi-language content (English, Zulu, Xhosa, Afrikaans)
- Media attachments (images, videos)
- Compliance page accessibility
- Cross-province scenarios
- Sponsored content perception

**Success Criteria:**
- 70%+ engage with multi-language content
- 60%+ successfully share media
- 80%+ find compliance pages clear
- 75%+ trust sponsored content labeling
- 65%+ understand regional targeting

**Duration:** 15 minutes  
**Steps:**
1. Receive invitation message
2. Send "START" to +27 65 829 5041

**Observation Points:**
- Time to complete subscription
- Confusion points in flow
- Understanding of commands
- Reaction to first Moment
- Web experience quality

---

### Scenario 2: Community Content Sharing
**Duration:** 10 minutes  
**Steps:**
1. User discovers local job opening
2. Messages details to +27 65 829 5041
3. Receives confirmation message
4. Content enters review queue
5. Admin reviews and approves
6. Content broadcast as Moment
7. User sees their contribution amplified

**Observation Points:**
- Willingness to share content
- Quality of submitted content
- Understanding of review process
- Satisfaction with attribution
- Repeat sharing behavior

---

### Scenario 3: Authority Request Flow
**Duration:** 20 minutes  
**Steps:**
1. User sends "REQUEST AUTHORITY"
2. Selects role (e.g., "Community Leader")
3. Provides institution name
4. Selects region
5. Receives confirmation
6. Admin reviews request
7. User receives approval notification
8. Accesses admin dashboard
9. Creates first Moment
10. Broadcasts to community

**Observation Points:**
- Clarity of multi-step process
- Drop-off points
- Time to admin review
- Dashboard usability
- First Moment quality

---

### Scenario 4: Sponsored Content Experience
**Duration:** 10 minutes  
**Steps:**
1. User receives sponsored Moment
2. Reads attribution block
3. Identifies sponsor clearly
4. Visits sponsor website link
5. Provides feedback on transparency

**Observation Points:**
- Trust in sponsored content
- Clarity of labeling
- Perception of value
- Sponsor attribution understanding
- Willingness to engage

---

### Scenario 5: Privacy & Compliance
**Duration:** 15 minutes  
**Steps:**
1. User visits privacy policy
2. Reviews data usage page
3. Checks unsubscribe process
4. Sends "STOP" command
5. Receives confirmation
6. Sends "START" to rejoin
7. Reviews contact page

**Observation Points:**
- Clarity of privacy information
- Trust in data handling
- Ease of unsubscribe
- Confidence in rejoining
- Support accessibility
