Data Usage & Retention

Complete transparency on what data we collect, how we use it, and how long we keep it.

Data We Collect

Data Type Purpose Retention Period
WhatsApp Phone Number Message delivery, subscription management While subscribed + 30 days after STOP
Province Preferences Send relevant local content While subscribed + 30 days after STOP
Interest Categories Filter content by topics you care about While subscribed + 30 days after STOP
Subscription Status Track opt-in/opt-out While subscribed + 30 days after STOP
Messages You Send Content moderation, potential broadcast 90 days or until broadcasted
Broadcast Logs (Aggregate) Service analytics, no individual tracking 12 months

How We Use Your Data

  • Message Delivery: Send moments to your WhatsApp based on your preferences
  • Content Filtering: Match content to your province and interests
  • Service Improvement: Understand what content serves communities best (aggregate only)
  • Moderation: Review submitted content for safety and relevance
  • Compliance: Meet legal requirements under POPIA and WhatsApp policies

What We DON'T Do

  • Never sell your data to third parties
  • Never track individual reading behavior
  • Never share your phone number with sponsors or partners
  • Never use your data for advertising outside our service

Data Retention Schedule

Active Subscribers

  • All subscription data retained while you're opted in
  • Messages you send retained for 90 days or until processed
  • No automatic deletion while subscribed

After Unsubscribing (STOP Command)

  • Day 0: Subscription marked as inactive
  • Day 1-30: Data retained for potential re-subscription
  • Day 30: All personal data permanently deleted
  • Aggregate stats: Retained (no personal identifiers)

Broadcast Analytics

  • Total subscribers count: Retained indefinitely
  • Broadcast success rates: 12 months
  • Regional engagement: 12 months (aggregate only)
  • Individual delivery logs: 30 days

Data Security

We protect your data using:

  • Encryption: All data encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Access Controls: Limited staff access, role-based permissions
  • Secure Infrastructure: Supabase (SOC 2 compliant)
  • Regular Audits: Security reviews and updates
  • WhatsApp Business API: Meta's secure messaging platform

Your Rights (POPIA)

Under South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act, you can:

  • Access: Request what data we have (email info@unamifoundation.org)
  • Correction: Update preferences anytime via WhatsApp commands
  • Deletion: Send "STOP" for immediate opt-out + 30-day deletion
  • Portability: Request your data in readable format
  • Objection: Object to any data processing

Third-Party Services

We use these services to operate Moments:

  • WhatsApp Business API (Meta): Message delivery - Privacy Policy
  • Supabase: Database and storage - Privacy Policy
