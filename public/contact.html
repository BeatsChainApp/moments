Frequently Asked Questions

How do I subscribe?

Send "START" to +27 65 829 5041 on WhatsApp. See our subscription guide.

Is it free?

Yes! The service is completely free. Standard WhatsApp data rates apply.

How do I unsubscribe?

Send "STOP" to +27 65 829 5041. See our unsubscribe guide.

What data do you collect?

Only your phone number and preferences. Read our Privacy Policy and Data Usage page.

Can I share opportunities?

Yes! Message us directly with local opportunities, events, or important community information.

How often will I receive messages?

Typically 1-5 messages per week, depending on community activity in your area.
