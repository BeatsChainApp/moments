Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request authority via WhatsApp?

Send "REQUEST AUTHORITY" to +27 65 829 5041. Our system will guide you through the application process. Admin approval takes 24-48 hours.

What if I exceed my blast radius?

You can upgrade to sponsored content for unlimited reach. Contact our admin team to discuss pricing and campaign options.

How much does sponsored content cost?

R0.45-0.60 per conversation (24-hour window). Batch 100+ messages in one window = R0.006 per recipient. No platform fees.

What's a "conversation window"?

A 24-hour period where you can send unlimited messages to a recipient. One conversation charge covers all messages in that window.

Can I target specific regions?

Yes! Both organic and sponsored partners can target specific provinces, regions, or categories for precise community reach.

How do I access sponsored campaign management?

Sponsored campaigns are admin-controlled. Contact info@unamifoundation.org to discuss your campaign needs and our team will manage the setup and execution.

What content is prohibited?

No spam, promotional content (organic only), commercial advertising (organic only), or POPIA violations. Sponsored content must be community-focused.

Can I upgrade from organic to sponsored later?

Absolutely! Contact our admin team anytime to discuss sponsored campaign options and pricing.
