How to Subscribe

Join thousands of South Africans receiving community moments via WhatsApp.

Quick Start

Step 1: Send "START" to +27 65 829 5041 on WhatsApp

Step 2: Choose your province (e.g., "KZN", "WC", "GP")

Step 3: Select your interests (Education, Safety, Jobs, etc.)

Step 4: Start receiving community moments!

What You're Consenting To

By sending "START", you explicitly consent to:

  • Receive WhatsApp messages from Unami Foundation Moments
  • Data collection of your phone number and preferences
  • Content delivery based on your selected province and interests
  • Occasional sponsored content (clearly labeled)

What We'll Send You

  • Local opportunities and job postings
  • Community events and cultural activities
  • Safety updates and important notices
  • Educational resources and training programs
  • Health and wellness information

Frequency

You'll receive messages based on community activity in your area. Typically:

  • 1-5 messages per week for active communities
  • More during high-activity periods (events, opportunities)
  • Less during quiet periods

Important: Your Control

You can unsubscribe anytime by sending "STOP" to +27 65 829 5041

No questions asked. Your data will be deleted within 30 days.

Free to use. Standard WhatsApp data rates apply.

Your Privacy

We collect minimal data and never sell your information. Read our full Privacy Policy.

Commands You Can Use

  • START - Subscribe to moments
  • STOP - Unsubscribe from moments
  • HELP - Get help and see all commands
  • REGIONS - Change your province preferences
  • INTERESTS - Update your topic preferences
