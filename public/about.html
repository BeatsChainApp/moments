About Unami Foundation Moments

Our Mission

Unami Foundation Moments empowers South African communities through accessible, privacy-respecting, WhatsApp-native engagement. We connect neighbors with local opportunities, events, and important community information.

What We Do

We provide a free community notice board delivered directly to your WhatsApp. No app downloads, no complicated signups—just simple, relevant updates about what matters in your community.
